Site Plan – Little Tales Library

Site Name

Little Tales Library

This name reflects a digital space where children can enjoy simple, interactive, and bilingual stories. It is friendly, memorable, and directly tied to storytelling for young audiences.

Optional domain availability: littletaleslibrary.org

Site Purpose

The site provides a small collection of interactive children’s stories in both English and Spanish. It encourages reading and language learning through short, engaging stories with visuals and simple user interaction. The goal is to support early literacy and bilingual development in a playful digital environment.

Scenarios

  • Can I read short bilingual stories with my child on this site?
  • Are the stories suitable for early readers or language learners?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #6C63FF – used for headings and buttons

Accent Color: #FFD166 – used for highlights, links, and story cards

Typography

  • Poppins – Used for headings and titles
  • Open Sans – Used for body text and paragraphs
